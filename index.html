About

MINE, one of the characters from Matt Furie's book "The Night Rider", is a mole who meets Hoppy, Wat, and Flappy, adding a delightful touch of whimsy to their adventures. In the story, before the frog and the rat meet the bat, they encountered the dragon's subterranean friends. Including MINE, a sleepy mole hibernating near the bat's basement. Though being a side character, readers easily recognized MINE, with his funny and attactive colors and hairy skin.

How to buy

Make Wallet

download phantom or your wallet of choice from the app store or google play store for free. Desktop users, download the google chrome extension by going to phantom.app.

Get Some SOL

have SOL in your wallet to switch to $MINE. If you don’t have any SOL, you can buy directly on phantom, transfer from another wallet, or buy on another exchange and send it to your wallet.

Go To pump.fun

connect to pump.fun. Go to pump.fun in google chrome or on the browser inside your Phantom app. Connect your wallet. Paste the $MINE token address into pump.fun, select $MINE COIN, and confirm. When Phantom prompts you for a wallet signature, sign.

Switch SOL for $MINE

switch SOL for $MINE. We have a 0% BUY/SELL TAX so try to set your slippage to 6%-10%.
